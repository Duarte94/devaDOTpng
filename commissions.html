ABOUT COMMISSIONS

These artwork are created for personal use only. You cannot reproduce them for finnancial gain.
If you want to use the illustration for a Youtube channel, Twitch, sell it events or something that goes beyond personal use will have an additional fee.
I usually take 1~3 weeks to finish a commission. If I see that it takes longer, I will let you know.
If you need the commission for an specific date, we can discuss it and get a rush fee.

PROCESS

Work in progress (sketch) will be delivered ffirst to check if everything is OK to continue with the process.
Changes can be done in the WIP step. If changes are big, there will be an additional fee.
Please be specific with your commission description toavoid these inconveniences.
After the sketch and base color being approved, I will continue with your commission until I finish.

PAYMENT

Payment through PAYPAL.
Once the commission starts there is no refund, so please stay aware of this.
There will be FULL REFUND only if for some reason I can not work on your commission anymore.
A small additional fee applies for the PayPal commission. (Detailed in invoice)
Payment is upfront. I will add you to waitlist and I will start your commission after payment is done.
